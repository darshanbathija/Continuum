Continuum — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 June 2026 · Continuum Console, by Montauk Analytics Inc.

Continuum ("the app") is a usage dashboard for AI coding tools (Claude Code, Codex, and similar CLIs). This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: Continuum does not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal data.

Data we collect

None. Montauk Analytics Inc operates no servers that receive your data. The app does not include analytics SDKs, advertising, trackers, or crash-reporting that sends data to us.

How the app works (all on your devices)

  • Local usage data. The app reads usage logs and rate-limit information that the AI CLIs already store locally on your Mac (e.g. under your home directory) to compute the gauges and analytics you see. This data is processed on-device and is not sent to us.
  • Device-to-device sync. When you pair your Mac, iPhone, and Apple Watch, data moves directly between your own devices over your local network / a Tailscale connection or an end-to-end-encrypted relay. We cannot read paired content.
  • iCloud. Optional Mac→iPhone sync uses your own iCloud account (Apple's iCloud Key-Value store / Keychain). It is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours.
  • Your AI provider credentials. To show live rate-limit status, the app may call Anthropic or OpenAI endpoints using your own API token, stored only in your device Keychain. These requests go directly from your device to those providers under their respective policies; they do not pass through Montauk.

Data sharing

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any.

Children

The app is rated 4+ and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Your control

Because all data stays on your devices, deleting the app removes its on-device data. Paired-device and iCloud data is managed through your own device and iCloud settings.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new version will be posted at this URL.
