Continuum is your unified workspace for AI chat, coding agents, and usage analytics — on iPhone, Apple Watch, and Mac, kept in sync.

CHAT — one place for every model
Talk to Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, Grok, and OpenRouter from a single chat. Broadcast one prompt to several models at once, compare their answers side by side, then continue with the one you like best.

CODE — drive your coding agents from anywhere
Start, monitor, and steer Claude Code, Codex, and other CLI coding agents running on your Mac — right from your iPhone or Apple Watch. Review diffs and pull requests, approve plans, watch live terminals, and reply by voice. Your phone becomes a remote control for your dev machine.

ANALYTICS — never get surprised by a limit
Live rate-limit gauges for Claude Code and Codex show how much of your 5-hour and weekly limits you've used. Historical token and cost analytics break spend down by project and model, with Apple Watch complications that put it all on your wrist.
